# Aether power plan

## Major components
### Processor - STM32L100
Operating voltage: 1.8 - 3.6 V  
Power dissapation: 444 W max  
Current consumption:
- Run: 11 mA

### GPS - Venus838LPx-L
Operating voltage: 2.8 - 3.6 V  
Current consumption:
- Enhanced acquisition: 45 mA
- Low power acquisition: 30 mA
- Tracking: 27 mA

Backup voltage: 2.5 - 3.6 V
Backup current: 40 uA

### Transceiver - Si4463
Operating voltage: 1.8 - 3.6 V  
Current consumption:
- Shutdown: 30 nA
- Standby: 50 nA
- Ready: 1.8 mA
- RX High: 14 mA
- TX: 75 mA
